UNDERSTAND AND ALIGN TO THE LAB RULES AND POLICIES

Lab Rules & Policies

  • Respect for Others: Be courteous and respectful towards fellow users in the lab. No loud talking or disruptive behavior.
  • Cleanliness: Keep your workstation clean. Dispose of trash properly. Food and beverages are not allowed near the computers or other equipment.
  • Time Limit: Each student is allowed to use a workstation for a maximum of 2 hours during peak hours. Please log out if you are done using the computer so others can use it.
  • Software Installation: Do not install any software on the computers unless authorized by lab administrators.
  • Data Privacy: Ensure that you log out of all accounts and close all files before leaving the workstation.
  • Use of Resources: The lab is primarily for academic purposes. Use of computers for gaming, social media, or non-academic activities is prohibited.
  • Report Issues: If you notice any technical issues with the computers or equipment, report them to the lab assistant immediately.
  • Security and Safety: Ensure your belongings are safe. The lab is not responsible for lost or stolen items. No disruptive activities that can damage equipment or disrupt others.
  • Backup Data: Always back up your work to a cloud service or external storage device. The lab is not responsible for lost data.
  • Lab Timings: The lab is open from 8:00 AM to 22:00 PM. Please ensure that you respect the timings.
